"Petrified" is a project inspired by petrified wood that I saw at the American Museum of Natural History. Petrified wood forms when a slice of wood turns into stone after being buried for millions of years. Burial protects the wood from decay while groundwater permeates its open spaces, depositing minerals and preserving the wood’s outer and inner structures. The project is an abstract exploration of the relationship between one and the other, depicting how the external transforms into the other.

The project employs the Face Expression Recognition API from face-api.js to recognize and analyze the user's facial expressions captured via webcam. It integrates backgrounds featuring images of emotional resonance. This experimentation aims to study the dynamics of the user's facial expressions and the corresponding emotional states identified through the Face Expression Recognition technology.
